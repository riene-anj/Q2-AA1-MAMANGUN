The yellow lines on the other hand correspond to the EEZ or Exclusive Economic Zone of the Philippines. What does the Exclusive Economic Zone mean? What is its significance? Well the EEZ corresponds to the 200 nautical-mile regions from the territory itself. The country claims legal rights for it since this area is the West Philippine Sea...leading to the ability to use its resources. Since our EEZ is within China's 9-dash-line, both parties claim over the territory since the area of ownership clashes as seen in this map.
